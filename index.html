Our goal is to enable 1 million girls to stay in education through the power of cycles by 2030.

Step 1:

Donate any unused cycles

We collect donated cycles and repair them.

Donate a cycle

Step 2:

Working with rural schools

With the help of rural secondary schools, we identify girls who would benefit from these refurbished cycles.

Step 3:

Riding and training

We host workshops & practical sessions for the girls, teaching them how to ride on the road as well as cycle maintenance skills.

Support Us

Step 4:

Gifting them the cycles

The girls receive more than the cycles, they also earn independence and a way to complete their education.

Step 5:

Gifting them the cycles

Once they finish school, the girls return the cycle to the school ready to empower the next student.
